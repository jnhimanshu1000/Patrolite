About Institute
Patronage Edubuzz is an apex institution and provides professional coaching for Income Tax, Auditing, Accountancy, Financial Management, Economics and experienced faculty members.

The institute provides a professional, unique, innovative and progressive learning environment to the students which help them to choose the right path to achieve their goals and targets.
Vision
Our vision is to provide students a destination where they could get a different class of education besides experiencing their overall development which assists them to realize their potential to fulfill their desired goals.
Mission
Our mission is to create an excellent competitive environment in student community by virtue of imparting qualitative education with consolidated strategy and scientific approach to produce proficient professionals and to provide the students an education of high standards which could cater their ever-changing needs. Our primary objective lies in the development of every student. We believe that an institute's performance depends on the foundation of a skilled, efficient & a visionary team. So, we always search and try to come up with the best possible team for ensuring the excellence to align the needs of the aspiring students.
